常见使用方式

  • 新增联系人：进入联系人页，点击右上角加号，手动创建你想维护的人。
  • 导入通讯录：进入联系人页，选择“导入通讯录”，授权后勾选你真正想保存到 App 的联系人。
  • 设置关系分：在联系人编辑页为联系人设置 1-10 的手动关系评分。
  • 记录一次联系：在联系人详情或记录页添加电话、微信聊天、聚餐、散步等联系记录。
  • 安排联系计划：在联系人详情或待联系页创建下一次联系计划，到点后通过本地通知提醒。

常见问题

  • 导入通讯录时看不到联系人：请确认已在 iOS 系统设置中为“我们”开启通讯录权限。
  • 没有收到提醒：请确认你已在应用内开启本地提醒，并在系统设置中允许“我们”发送通知。
  • 待联系状态没有变化：新增或补录一次联系记录后，最近联系时间和关系状态会自动刷新。
  • 关系分会自动变化吗：不会。1-10 的关系分由你手动维护，联系记录只影响最近联系时间和提醒状态。
  • 数据会同步到云端吗：当前版本不提供账号、云备份、iCloud 同步或多设备同步。

排查建议

  • 先检查系统权限：通讯录权限影响导入，通知权限影响提醒。
  • 再检查联系人是否已经保存到 App 内；未确认导入的人不会被保存。
  • 如果刚刚修改过提醒时间、联系计划或联系人状态，可以进入“设置 - 提醒与通知”刷新提醒。
  • 如果应用无法打开本地数据，请先不要删除应用，重启设备后再尝试打开。

Common Tasks

  • Add a contact: Open the Contacts tab, tap the plus button, and create a person you want to maintain.
  • Import contacts: Choose Import Contacts, grant permission, then select only the people you want to save into the app.
  • Set a relationship score: Use the contact editor to set a manual 1-10 score.
  • Record an interaction: Add a phone call, WeChat chat, meal, walk, or other interaction from the contact detail or activity page.
  • Plan the next contact: Create a contact plan from a contact detail page or the follow-up list, then receive a local reminder at the planned time.

FAQ

  • I cannot see contacts during import: Make sure Contacts permission is enabled for We in iOS system settings.
  • I did not receive reminders: Make sure local reminders are enabled in the app and notifications are allowed in system settings.
  • Follow-up status did not change: After adding or backfilling an interaction record, the last contact time and relationship status are refreshed.
  • Does the relationship score change automatically: No. The 1-10 relationship score is maintained manually by you. Interaction records affect last contact time and reminder status only.
  • Is data synced to the cloud: No. The current version does not provide accounts, cloud backup, iCloud sync, or multi-device sync.

Troubleshooting

  • Check system permissions first: Contacts permission affects import, and notification permission affects reminders.
  • Confirm that the contact has been saved into the app. People not confirmed for import are not saved.
  • If you recently changed reminder time, contact plans, or contact status, refresh reminders from Settings - Reminders & Notifications.
  • If local data cannot be opened, please do not delete the app first. Restart the device and try opening the app again.
