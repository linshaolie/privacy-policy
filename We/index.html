我们 App 隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 5 月 19 日

“我们”是一款帮助用户记录联系人、联系节奏、关系评分、联系计划和本地提醒的 iOS 应用。我们重视你的人际关系数据和个人隐私。本政策说明“我们”在当前版本中会如何处理数据、请求权限，以及你可以如何管理这些数据。

当前版本以本地使用为主，不提供账号登录、云同步、广告追踪或第三方分析服务。

中文 English

1. 我们处理哪些信息

为了提供联系人管理、关系维护和提醒功能，“我们”可能会在你的设备本地处理以下信息：

  • 你主动创建或编辑的联系人信息：例如姓名、手机号、微信号、标签、头像、备注、生日、关系描述等。
  • 你主动记录的联系信息：例如联系时间、联系方式、联系备注和历史记录。
  • 你设置的关系维护信息：例如 1-10 的手动关系评分、提醒阈值、待联系状态、联系计划和提醒偏好。
  • 应用设置：例如语言偏好、通知开关、提醒时间和首次导入提示状态。

2. 通讯录权限

当你主动使用“导入通讯录”功能时，应用会向系统请求通讯录权限。获得权限后，应用会读取系统通讯录中的部分字段，用于展示可选择的导入预览和帮助识别重复联系人。

当前版本可能读取的通讯录字段包括：联系人姓名、昵称、电话号码、公司、职位和缩略头像。只有你勾选并确认导入的联系人，才会写入“我们”的本地数据库。未被选择的联系人不会被保存为应用内联系人。

“我们”不会把系统通讯录自动完整同步到应用内，也不会将通讯录数据上传到服务器。

3. 头像与图片

你可以为联系人设置头像。头像可能来自你手动选择的图片，也可能来自你确认导入的系统通讯录缩略头像。头像数据会和联系人资料一起保存在当前设备本地，用于联系人展示。

当前版本不会将头像上传到服务器，也不会把头像用于广告、画像或第三方分析。

4. 通知权限

当你开启本地提醒时，应用会向系统请求通知权限。通知只用于关系维护提醒和你手动创建的联系计划提醒。

为了保护隐私，当前版本的通知文案会尽量保持克制，不在通知正文中展示完整关系备注或敏感细节。你可以随时在应用内或系统设置中关闭通知权限。

5. 数据如何保存

当前版本的数据主要保存在你的设备本地。联系人、联系记录、关系评分、标签、联系计划、头像和提醒偏好等信息会存储在本地数据库或本地设置中，以便离线查看和编辑。

当前版本不提供账号系统、云端备份、iCloud 同步或多设备同步。删除应用可能会导致本地数据一并被删除，请在删除前确认你不再需要这些数据。

6. 我们不会做什么

  • 不会自动读取微信、短信、电话记录或其他社交应用内容。
  • 不会替你拨打电话、发送短信、发送微信或向他人发送消息。
  • 不会将你的联系人、联系记录、备注、关系评分或头像用于广告画像。
  • 不会接入第三方广告 SDK 或第三方分析 SDK。
  • 不会出售你的个人数据。

7. 你如何管理数据与权限

  • 编辑或删除数据：你可以在应用内编辑或删除联系人、联系记录和联系计划。删除后，相关本地数据和本地提醒会随之更新。
  • 关闭通讯录权限：你可以在 iOS 系统设置中关闭“我们”的通讯录权限。关闭后，应用将无法继续读取系统通讯录用于导入预览。
  • 关闭通知权限：你可以在应用内关闭本地提醒，或在 iOS 系统设置中关闭通知权限。
  • 删除应用：如果你删除应用，iOS 通常会同时删除应用在本机保存的数据。

8. 儿童隐私

“我们”面向希望管理和维护人际关系的普通用户，并非专门面向儿童设计。我们不会有意收集儿童个人信息。如果你认为应用中保存了不应保存的信息，请通过本政策末尾的联系方式与我们联系。

9. 政策更新

随着功能变化，我们可能会更新本隐私政策。例如未来如果加入账号、云同步、备份或第三方服务，我们会在政策中说明新增的数据处理方式，并在必要时请求新的权限或征得你的同意。

We App Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 19, 2026

We is an iOS app that helps users record contacts, contact rhythms, relationship scores, contact plans, and local reminders. We respect your relationship data and personal privacy. This policy explains how We handles data, requests permissions, and lets you manage your data in the current version.

The current version is local-first. It does not provide account login, cloud sync, advertising tracking, or third-party analytics.

中文 English

1. Information We Process

To provide contact management, relationship maintenance, and reminder features, We may process the following information locally on your device:

  • Contact information you create or edit: such as name, phone number, WeChat ID, tags, avatar, notes, birthday, and relationship descriptions.
  • Interaction records you create: such as contact time, contact method, interaction notes, and history.
  • Relationship maintenance information you set: such as manual 1-10 relationship scores, reminder thresholds, follow-up status, contact plans, and reminder preferences.
  • App settings: such as language preference, notification switch, reminder time, and first-import prompt state.

2. Contacts Permission

When you actively use the Import Contacts feature, the app asks the system for Contacts permission. After permission is granted, the app reads selected fields from your system contacts to show an import preview and help identify duplicate contacts.

The current version may read contact fields including name, nickname, phone number, organization, job title, and thumbnail avatar. Only contacts that you select and confirm for import are written into We's local database. Contacts you do not select are not saved as in-app contacts.

We does not automatically sync your entire system address book into the app, and it does not upload your contacts data to a server.

3. Avatars and Images

You may set an avatar for a contact. The avatar may come from an image you manually choose or from a system contact thumbnail that you confirm for import. Avatar data is stored locally on the current device together with the contact profile and is used for contact display.

The current version does not upload avatars to a server and does not use avatars for advertising, profiling, or third-party analytics.

4. Notifications Permission

When you enable local reminders, the app asks the system for notification permission. Notifications are used only for relationship maintenance reminders and contact plan reminders you create manually.

To protect privacy, notification copy in the current version is intentionally restrained and avoids showing full relationship notes or sensitive details in notification bodies. You may disable notifications at any time in the app or in system settings.

5. How Data Is Stored

Data in the current version is mainly stored locally on your device. Contacts, interaction records, relationship scores, tags, contact plans, avatars, and reminder preferences are stored in a local database or local settings so that you can view and edit them offline.

The current version does not provide accounts, cloud backup, iCloud sync, or multi-device sync. Deleting the app may also delete data stored locally by the app. Please make sure you no longer need the data before deleting the app.

6. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not automatically read WeChat, SMS, phone call history, or content from other social apps.
  • We do not make phone calls, send SMS, send WeChat messages, or message others on your behalf.
  • We do not use your contacts, interaction records, notes, relationship scores, or avatars for advertising profiles.
  • We do not integrate third-party advertising SDKs or third-party analytics SDKs.
  • We do not sell your personal data.

7. How You Can Manage Data and Permissions

  • Edit or delete data: You can edit or delete contacts, interaction records, and contact plans in the app. After deletion, related local data and local reminders are updated accordingly.
  • Disable Contacts permission: You can disable Contacts permission for We in iOS system settings. After it is disabled, the app can no longer read system contacts for import previews.
  • Disable Notifications permission: You can turn off local reminders in the app or disable notification permission in iOS system settings.
  • Delete the app: If you delete the app, iOS usually deletes the data saved locally by the app as well.

8. Children's Privacy

We is intended for general users who want to manage and maintain personal relationships. It is not designed specifically for children. We do not knowingly collect children's personal information. If you believe that the app stores information that should not be stored, please contact us using the contact information at the end of this policy.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as features change. For example, if future versions add accounts, cloud sync, backups, or third-party services, we will explain the new data processing practices in this policy and request new permissions or obtain your consent where required.
