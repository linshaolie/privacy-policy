核心功能

联系人管理

新增、编辑、搜索、标签筛选，也可以从系统通讯录中选择部分联系人导入。

手动关系评分

用 1-10 分表达你对一段关系重要程度的主观判断，分数由你自己维护。

联系记录

记录电话、微信聊天、聚餐、散步等互动，让最近一次联系时间清晰可见。

待联系列表

太久未联系的人会进入待联系列表，帮你知道今天可以从谁开始。

本地提醒

为关系维护和联系计划设置克制的本地通知，不暴露过多关系细节。

本地优先

当前版本以本机保存为主，不接入广告追踪或第三方分析。

适合谁使用

  • 想认真维护亲友、同学、合作伙伴、客户或导师关系的人。
  • 经常想起“应该联系一下谁”，但容易拖延或忘记的人。
  • 希望知道上次什么时候联系过某个人，并保留互动脉络的人。
  • 希望把关系维护变成可持续小习惯，而不是临时想起的压力的人。

隐私与克制

当前版本不提供账号、云同步、广告追踪或第三方分析。通讯录只在你主动导入时读取用于预览，只有你勾选并确认的人会保存到 App 内。

联系记录、关系评分、标签、头像、联系计划和提醒偏好主要保存在当前设备本地。

A gentle relationship maintenance companion

Keep important people in sight. Record contact rhythms, relationship scores, and follow-up reminders in a calm, personal way.

We is not a sales CRM, and it does not decide who matters for you. It is a quiet relationship notebook that helps you notice, record, and act while keeping the decision in your hands.

中文 English Privacy Policy Support

Key Features

Contact management

Add, edit, search, filter by tags, and import selected people from system contacts.

Manual relationship scores

Use a 1-10 score to express your own sense of relationship importance. The score is maintained by you.

Interaction records

Record phone calls, WeChat chats, meals, walks, and other interactions so the last contact time is clear.

Follow-up list

People you have not contacted for a while appear in a focused follow-up list so you know where to start.

Local reminders

Set restrained local notifications for relationship maintenance and contact plans without exposing too many details.

Local-first

The current version is mainly stored on device and does not integrate advertising tracking or third-party analytics.

Who It Is For

  • People who want to maintain friends, family, classmates, collaborators, clients, mentors, or partners with intention.
  • People who often think "I should reach out" but forget or delay it.
  • People who want to know when they last contacted someone and keep a lightweight interaction history.
  • People who want relationship maintenance to become a sustainable habit instead of occasional pressure.

Privacy and Restraint

The current version does not provide accounts, cloud sync, advertising tracking, or third-party analytics. System contacts are read only when you actively import contacts for preview, and only people you select and confirm are saved into the app.

Interaction records, relationship scores, tags, avatars, contact plans, and reminder preferences are mainly stored locally on the current device.
